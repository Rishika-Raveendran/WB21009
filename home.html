BLISSFUL BEACHES

Trivandrum is home to a number of spectacular beaches that will take your breath away. As Trivandrum is a coastal area, some of the most popular tourist destinations in Trivandrum are beaches like Kovalam,Shanghumugham, Poovar and the list goes on and on. It replete with exotic resorts and hotels where travellers can spend their time sunbathing, swimming or simply enjoying the stunning view.

WORLD CLASS MUSEUMS

Museums are an essential tool for preserving and restoring our rich cultural and artistic past. The legacies of a given era - artwork, paintings, sculptures and even entire cultures can be preserved in a museum. This preservation is significant to ensure that upcoming generations are rooted in their history to learn and grow from their ancestral past. The Museums in Trivandrum hope to achieve something on similar lines - to preserve the cultural and heritage past of the state of Kerala and city Trivandrum for its growing population.

ANCIENT STRUCTURES

The capital city of Kerala, Thiruvananthapuram or Trivandrum is an appealing blend of a strongly rooted heritage and a nostalgic colonial legacy. This heritage dates back centuries, showcasing temples and religious centres.

EXOTIC HILL STATIONS

Locals in the capital city of Thiruvananthapuram will declare that a weekend drive to Ponmudi is among the best ways to spend time in the city. Families and friends alike are known to go on long drives up the winding paths for a view they insist rivals any other in the State. The slopes shrouded in mist fill one with a feeling of wonder and amazement. A large amount of rare flora and fauna can be seen here including mountain flowers, wild orchids and exotic butterflies

Every inch defining beauty

Trivandrum means city of gold. It is considered as one of the devotional cities of the state. At the same time it is also one of the 2 commercial cities of Kerala besides Cochin. Trivandrum also refers to the city of the Lord where it is believed to be one of the most prosperous cities in the state. Being a coastal area, the temperatures are hot and humid throughout the course of the year except the month of November-December. It is also one of the most populous places in Kerala. The seafood is very tasty, just behind Kozhikode.Called the ‘Evergreen city of India’, Thiruvananthapuram is the capital of the state of Kerala. The city comprises beautiful beaches, long stretches of palm fringed shorelines, windy backwaters and historical structures. Along with its natural and historical significance, the city is also popular for its ancient temples that are renowned for their architecture making Thiruvananthapuram one of the most visited pilgrim centers of India.
It also has a commercial city where it has a rich source of tech companies as there are a lot of IT companies and events which take place in Trivandrum. It is also said to be one of the cleanest cities in which speaks volume about the administration in this city along with mindset of the people. The Lord Padmanabha Swamy temple is one of the most ancient and holy temples in India. It is believed to be a very powerful temple as many people believe by performing various ceremonies, the temple helps in bringing rain as well as reducing rain for the calendar year. Being a coastal area, you would expect the place to have a lot of beaches which is exactly what it is. Kovalam beach is nearby from the Trivandrum city and hence is easier to travel there.
